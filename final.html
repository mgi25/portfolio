Portfolio

This chart highlights a key trading concept known as the Quasimodo Line (QML). The QML is identified after a significant event called the Change of Character (CHoC), where the market shifts direction from an uptrend to a downtrend. Once this shift occurs, the QML serves as a critical level where traders can anticipate and target the next lower low.

The chart demonstrates the Trend Joining Level (TJL), a crucial point used for entering a position within an ongoing trend. The TJL represents an optimal entry point for traders looking to join a trend at a favorable price level. This strategy allows traders to enter with precision, maximizing profit while minimizing risk.
